Tropical and Exotic Fruits

Passion Fruit (Maracuja)

Country:

Brazil

Characteristics:

Small, round or oval fruit with a tough outer rind and juicy, seed-filled interior.

Vitamins and Minerals:

  • Vitamin C
  • Vitamin A
  • dietary fiber
  • iron
  • potassium

Advantages:

  • Boosts immune system
  • Aids digestion
  • Reduces anxiety
  • Improves insulin sensitivity

Disadvantages:

  • High in sugar; excessive consumption can lead to weight gain
  • May cause allergic reactions in some people

Planting:

Grows in tropical and subtropical climates. Needs well-drained soil and full sun.

Recipes:

  • Passion fruit juice
  • Mousse
  • Ice cream
  • Desserts
